A New Manager for the Community Bank

Apr 16, 2021 | News | 0

The Bank Team (L to R) Sean (Customer Relationship Manager) Kate (Customer Service Officer), Michelle (Customer Service Officer) Rachel (Customer Service Officer), Melanie (Branch Manager) Absent - Amanda (on leave)

Melanie Tudball has recently joined the Daylesford District Community Bank as Branch Manager.

Melanie has many years’ experience in finance and has been employed with Bendigo Bank for almost 7 years. She has most recently worked at the Bacchus Marsh and Ballan Community Bank, both highly successful Community Bank branches.

New branch manager, Melanie Tudball.

“I’m excited to join the team at the Daylesford District Community Bank and the prospect of working in a vibrant and active community such as Daylesford & Hepburn, really caught my attention. I love good coffee, great food and talking to interesting people, so where better to enjoy all three than here”.

Melanie has been a community minded person all her adult life and has held numerous roles as a Committee member and volunteer in various community groups.

“I appreciate the challenges faced by community groups, such as funding and volunteer involvement and in this COVID environment, keeping the sense of community alive”.

“I’ve also seen firsthand how the collaboration between the Bank and the Community can work, creating a great partnership that benefits the people of small communities. I believe there are great opportunities for the Daylesford District Community Bank to make a real contribution to community initiatives and projects and I’m keen to ensure the community is at the centre of our work.”

The Daylesford District Community Bank is a full service bank. They provide Business and consumer lending, transaction accounts, insurance, Superannuation, Wealth. Profits generated from the Community Bank are distributed back into the local community.
If you’d like to know more about the Community Bank model or Bendigo bank’s offering, Melanie can be contacted on 5348 4186.
