Alex and Emily at Yandoit Cultural

Jun 13, 2025 | Living well | 0

Local Daylesford couple, Emily Davis and Alex Tsibulski will perform their newest and favourite music at Yandoit Cultrual this coming Sunday afternoon, June 15th, at 3pm.

Emily, a singer-songwriter with three albums under her belt, is a performer of rare communicative ability and emotion. Classical guitar virtuoso Alex Tsiboulski is an award-winning performer and recording artist. Recently returned from a European playing, recording and teaching tour, he will be playing a selection of classical guitar favourites alongside fresh compositions by leading composers.

It promises to be a warm, intimate musical afternoon and a rare insight into a couple making music in different genres, bound by an admiration for each other’s emotional and musical journeys.

Date: Sunday June 15
Time: 3:00 pm to 4:45 pm
Venue: Yandoit Cultural, Church Rd, Yandoit VIC
Cost: $30/$20
Bookings: https://www.trybooking.com/events/landing/1409960
