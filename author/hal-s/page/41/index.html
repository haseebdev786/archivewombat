Potholes on the Mend

Sep 16, 2022 | News

The Victorian Government will invest $780 million to support an annual maintenance blitz on the state’s arterial roads, a doubling of funding from a decade ago. The program will rebuild, repair and resurface more than 365 kilometres of roads across central Victoria. Hepburn Shire Council is also investing in road repairs.
