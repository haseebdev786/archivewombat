Car Upgrades for Young People

Aug 19, 2022 | NewsNews

The Victorian Government is giving more young people in Daylesford and Hepburn Springs the chance to save money and ditch their older model cars for a safer, more modern vehicle. Up to 150 drivers across Regional Victoria, aged 18-25 years old, will take part in this phase of the program, which will provide a $5,000 subsidy to replace their unsafe and older vehicle with a safer, newer vehicle.
