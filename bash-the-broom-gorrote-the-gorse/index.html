Bash the Broom/ Garrotte the Gorse!

May 6, 2022 | News, Uncategorized | 0

It’s a never ending task on Cornish Hill. But with some help from our Friends we can make a difference.

Over the past decade, along the Smith Creek track, we have been planting native trees, weeding, fixing tree guards and brushcutting so the walking track remains useable.

Over the years many people have volunteered to help. Now it’s time for a few new people to put their hand up to preserve this beautiful part of Daylesford.

Do you live near the Hill? Do you walk along the tracks at the Hill? Do you value having some bush and it’s wildlife nearby? If the answer is YES then please come to our next “Wack a weed and Save a tree” work sessions.

1. Sunday May 15
2. Sunday June 19

Time: 10am – noon

Park at the end of Stanley st near the pumping station and walk down to the picnic table.

BYO gloves, water bottle, brushcutter,smile Etc.

Contact Jan for further details
