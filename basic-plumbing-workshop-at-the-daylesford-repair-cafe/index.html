Basic Plumbing Workshop at the Daylesford Repair Café

Apr 14, 2023 | News, Uncategorized | 0

Jason Alaba will answer your questions about DIY plumbing at the Daylesford Repair Café this Sunday.

Do you want to know how to change a tap washer? Or fix a leak? Or join pipes together? Come along to the Daylesford Repair Café this Sunday afternoon with your basic plumbing questions and learn some practical tips from Mr Fixit (and plumber), Julian Alaba.

What else can be fixed at the Repair Café?

Join us at the Repair Café between 1pm and 4pm on Sunday, April 16th and see what else can be repaired. Our team of fixers and supporters can sharpen knives, scissors and garden tools. We can mend clothes, cushions and soft toys. We can repair bicycles, prams and other wheeled items. And household appliances as well as electrical equipment can also be fixed.

And we have a ‘mending broken hearts table’ for those in need of a listening ear.

Café: Enjoy a cuppa, cake and conversation while you’re there.

We’re always looking for people who can sew and repair clothes, so let us know if you’d like to join the sewing repair team.

See you on Sunday for an afternoon of fixing and helping to keep things out of landfill.

When: Sunday April 16th, 1.30-3.30pm
Where: Victoria Park Pavilion, Ballan Road, Daylesford
Cost: Donation

For further information contact Veronica Baker (0428 180 200) or Danny Kinnear (0488 604 231). Or check out the Daylesford Repair Cafe Facebook page
