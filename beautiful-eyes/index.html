Beautiful Eyes

Feb 25, 2022 | Living well | 0

Child Eyes Portrait Look Face Face Mask Boy

Gordon Nightingale

Beautiful eyes above the mask
Reveal the inner soul at last.
Eyes of brown,
Eyes of blue,
Shining eyes of emerald hue.

Beautiful eyes looking at me.
Beautiful eyes are all I see.
Eyes that smile,
Eyes that cry,
Revealing secrets.
The eyes can’t lie.

Gordon Nightingale is an author and poet. Although no longer a Daylesford local, he continues to make occasional contributions to the Wombat Post.
