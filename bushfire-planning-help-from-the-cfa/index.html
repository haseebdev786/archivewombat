Bushfire Planning Help from the CFA

Dec 1, 2023 | News | 0

AFAC says that there is an increased risk of bushfires this spring compared to last year. (Photo: Laura Tidey)

Do you have a bushfire survival plan but you’re not sure it’s as good as it can be?

Do you need a bushfire survival plan but you don’t know where to start?

The CFA is here to help.

The media is full of predictions that this could be a very bad bushfire season. Three years of La Niña conditions have promoted forest and grassland growth and the fuel load is high. Even with the recent rains, a hot, dry spell could leave us with forests and grasslands just waiting for a spark.

Whether you already have a bushfire survival plan or you are yet to get started, a free 2-hour workshop at the Daylesford CFA will help you put together a plan that ticks all the right boxes.

CFA specialists will work with local residents in small groups to help you develop your plan.

Date: Thursday, December 7, 2023
Time: 7:00 pm
Venue: Daylesford Fire Station
Bookings: No bookings required. This is a FREE event.
More information: Other workshops are available. Visit the CFA website for more information.
