Call for Local Businesses to Get on Board with an Outdoor Dining Gift Card

Aug 6, 2021 | News | 0

Hepburn Shire Council is inviting all local businesses who provide an outdoor dining experience to participate in a once-off initiative aimed at encouraging locals and visitors to make a $50 purchase at their business.

Council has partnered with Daylesford Macedon Tourism to provide a Gift Card Competition to encourage outdoor dining in businesses across the Shire. This program is a COVID-19 recovery initiative with funding support provided by the Victorian State Government. It has been designed to support local dining businesses who have been hit hard by recent lockdowns.

Eight hundred $50 gift cards will be given away to be spent in Hepburn Shire dining businesses. A further two hundred will be divided among eligible local participating businesses to promote or market their own business (for example through competitions or raffles) or to spend at another participating business.

Mayor, Cr Lesley Hewitt, said that the gift card program provides much needed support to local dining businesses across the Shire including restaurants, cafes and takeaways.
