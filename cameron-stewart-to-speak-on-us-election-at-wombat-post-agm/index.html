Cameron Stewart to Speak on US Election at Wombat Post AGM

Nov 15, 2024 | Uncategorized, News | 0

Cameron Stewart

Cameron Stewart, who is the Chief International Correspondent to The Australian and who has covered the last three US presidential elections will be the guest speaker at the Annual General Meeting of the Daylesford and District Community News Association. The DDCNA is the publisher of The Wombat Post.

Cameron is an expert in US politics having twice been a US correspondent for The Australian, earlier as New York correspondent during the late 1990s and again during the first Trump presidency in Washington from 2017 to 2021. He combines investigative reporting on foreign affairs, defence and national security with feature writing for the Weekend Australian Magazine. Cameron has won numerous awards for his work, including the Graham Perkin award for Australian Journalist of the Year.

Cameron has recently returned from the US where he crossed the country while covering Trump’s second election victory for The Australian.

The title for Cameron’s talk is “Donald Trump’s Comeback: What it means for America, Australia and the Media.” He will give his personal perspective about what has shaped American politics, the Trump phenomenon and what a Trump victory means for Australia and for the media.

The 2024 AGM of the DDCNA will be held on Wednesday, December 4th at 6:30 pm pm at the Victoria Park Pavilion, 3021 Ballan-Daylesford Rd, Daylesford.

The meeting is open to the public. Admission is free but bookings are essential for catering purposes at trybooking.

The meeting will be followed by light refreshments.
