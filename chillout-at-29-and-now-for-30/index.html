Chillout at 29 and now for 30

Mar 13, 2026 | Living well, Uncategorized | 0

Chillout had a great party for its 29th festival with all the usual glitz, glitter and pride brought together in a planetary bow. And next year the festival turns 30, which should be an event to remember. The Pride Parade, the events at Victoria Park and the shows and events around the town were a huge success as part of the Planet Love Celebrations.

Photos Chris Poropat and Julie Higgs

Along with everyone else Daylesford Shops got into the swing of the party with fabulous displays.

Chillout reports that the votes are in and the best displays were:

  • Clayfire Gallery
  • Hair and Beauty Retreat Daylesford
  • That Bargain Place

The best dressed staff were

  • That Bargain Place
  • Hair and Beauty Daylesford

The best exterior displays were:

  • Hair and Beauty Daylesford
  • Daylesford Spirit
  • Bendigo Bank
