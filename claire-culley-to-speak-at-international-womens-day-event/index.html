Claire Culley to Speak at International Women’s Day Event

Feb 25, 2022 | Uncategorized | 0

Claire Culley, CEO of the Otis Foundation will be the guest speaker at the IWD event.

Each year, the Shire pays pay tribute to the women in the Hepburn Shire whose contributions, courage and examples have led to significant social change for women. These women are recognised by induction into the Heather Mutimer Women’s Honour Roll.

This year, the induction ceremony will be held on Tuesday 8 March 2022 at the Daylesford Town Hall commencing at 6.00pm.

The reception will include the honour roll presentation and feature guest speaker Claire Culley, Chief Executive Officer for The Otis Foundation, a national not-for-profit organisation that provides retreat accommodation at no cost to anyone dealing with the challenges of breast cancer.

Victorian Government COVID Safe settings in place at the time will be observed. Attendees who are aged 18 years or older are required to show proof that they are fully vaccinated or have a valid medical exemption registered through the Australian Immunisation Register. (Note that regulations are changing and new requirements may be in place at the time of the event.)

To book a free ticket: https://www.trybooking.com/BXHPG. If you are unable to book a ticket online please call or drop into a Council customer service centre.
