Clean Sweep for Hepburn Netballers

Jul 9, 2021 | News, Sport and recreation | 0

Daylesford Bulldogs in action at Bungaree.

Daylesford Bulldogs lost a close encounter to Bungaree last weekend 5.9-39 to 6.9-45. Daylesford was on top for the first three quarters but fell away in the final quarter. Sam Hughes kicked two goals for the Bulldogs; John Butler kicked four for Bungaree. In the Reserves, Bungaree led all day to win 7.5-47 to 4.3-27. Rob Rodgers kicked two goals for Daylesford and Matt Lewis kicked two for Bungaree. Daylesford Under 12s and Under 18s both went down but the Under 15s won a lopsided match kicking 25.16-166 to 0.0-0. Tate Koleski kicked 7 goals for the Under 15s.

Hepburn Burras were way too good for Beaufort kicking 18.18-126 to 2.4-16. Andrew Mckay kicked 6 goals and Sam Dunstan kicked 5 for the Burras. The Reserves won a one-sided match 20.23-143 to no score with Jimmy Rogers and Adam Raak each kicking four goals. The Under 15s also held Beaufort scoreless while kicking 15.23-113.Nate Lowe kicked 8 goals and Mitch Yanner added 4 for Hepburn. The Beaufort Under 18s maintained some respectability winning 12.15-87 to 6.4-40. The Hepburn Under 12s won a low-scoring encounter 2.0-12 to 1.0-6.

Hepburn A Grade netballers easily defeated Beaufort 65-25. The B Grade team was well in control winning 38-28 as were the C Graders who won 51-13. The 17&Under team won 46-8, the 15&Under team won 59-1 and the 13&Under team won 33-8 to complete a clean sweep by the Hepburn netballers.

Daylesford B Grade netballers went down to Bungaree 39-31 and the C Grade team was outdone 42-5. The 17&Under team drew Beaufort 21-21, the 15&Under team lost 26-7 but the 13&Unders won 5-2.
