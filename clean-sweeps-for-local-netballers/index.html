Clean Sweeps for Local Netballers

Apr 30, 2021 | News, Sport and recreation | 0

Daylesford A-Grade netball Team had a comfortable win over Rokewood.

The Hepburn and Daylesford Football Netball Clubs both had clean sweeps of their netball fixtures last weekend with wins for both teams in all grades.

The Hepburn A-Grade side defeated Cargnham 100 to 16, the B-Grade side won 38-22 and the C-Grade side won 45-18. In age group results, the 17&Unders won 28-19, the 15&Unders won 45-6 and the 13&Unders won 28-9.

Daylesford also had a clean sweep of Rokewood with the B-Grade team winning 30-21 and the C-Grade side winning 32-24. The 17&Unders won 18-12, the 15&Unders won 9-8 and 13&Unders won 27-2.

In football results Hepburn Burras had a day out in their first home game of the season defeating Carngham Linton 39.13-247 to 5.2-32. Sam Dunstan put on a kicking clinic with 20 straight goals. The Reserves also had a big win but shared the goals around with Shane Black, Nathan Rogers, Adam Raak and Michael Chapman all kicking four goals. Carngham Linton had the upper hand in the under 18s winning 17.11-113 to 9.3-57. Hepburn under 15s had a thumping win but the under12s went down by 14 points.

Daylesford Bulldogs Seniors had a tough day at the office with the senior team losing to Rokewood Corindhap 5.13-43 to 14.11-95. Rokewood Corindhap had a huge wins in the Reserves and Under 18s. Daylesford won the under 15s in a very one-sided game and the under12s were also successful.

Daylesford is at home to Creswick and Hepburn is at home to Waubra this weekend.
