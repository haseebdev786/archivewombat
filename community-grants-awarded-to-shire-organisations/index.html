Community Grants Awarded to Shire Organisations

Apr 30, 2021 | News | 0

Daylesford Community Child Care Centre received a Community Grant to refresh playground equipment.

Council has distributed over $13,000 to organisations across the Shire to support organisations in their community work. This third round distribution is part of a total budget of $65,000 budgeted for distribution in four rounds over the 2020/21 financial year.

U3A Hepburn received $1000 to support an upcoming Zelman Orchestra Concert. Details of the concert will be available soon.

Daylesford Debutante Ball and the Daylesford Neighbourhood Centre received $2,500 to support a 2021 Debutante Ball to be held on August 14th at the Daylesford Town Hall.
