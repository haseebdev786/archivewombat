Community Support for Daylesford Hospital Upgrade Fast Tracks Master Plan

Sep 24, 2021 | Living well, News | 0

Presentation of a cheque from Rotary to Hospital Upgrade Fund. Left to right: Danny Moynihan, Alan Harrison, Jenny Hopkins, David Stagg, Patrick Groenhout (President of Rotary), John King, Kate Redwood, Cr Lesley Hewitt (Mayor).

An outpouring of generosity from the Daylesford community has resulted in the fast tracking of a Masterplan that will detail essential upgrades needed at the Daylesford Hospital.

The Daylesford Hospital Upgrade Community Campaign Committee has been overwhelmed by support since it went public recently with its target of $100,000 by Christmas to fund the masterplan.

An amazing $80, 000 has already been raised, just weeks into the campaign.

Donations have been made by individuals and groups within the community, with the Daylesford Community Bank matching every $3 donated with an additional $1.

The Rotary Club of Daylesford handed over a $5,000 cheque to Campaign Committee Chair, Cr Lesley Hewitt on Thursday morning, 24 September.

“We are thrilled with the community spirit that is driving the campaign to fund the Hospital Upgrade Masterplan,”Cr Hewitt said. “Once a Masterplan has been finalised, the Campaign Committee will seek commitments from the current state Government and Opposition as we lead into an election year in 2022.”

The Masterplan will set the priorities for the Hospital upgrade, including new sterilization facilities, an operating theatre rebuild, increased space for dialysis, shared rooms replaced with single rooms and improved disability access.

Contributions can be made by:

  • Cash/cheques/money order to Daylesford Community Bank – Daylesford Hospital Upgrade account.
  • Online: http://www.givenow.com.au/daylesfordhospitalupgrade

All donations over $2 are tax deductible.

For further comment please contact Daylesford Hospital Upgrade Community Campaign Committee Chair and Hepburn Shire Mayor Lesley Hewitt on 0407843928.
