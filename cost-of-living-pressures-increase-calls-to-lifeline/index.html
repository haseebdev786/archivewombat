Cost of Living Pressures Increase Calls to Lifeline

Apr 26, 2024 | Living well | 0

Cost of living pressures have been particularly acute in regional Victoria according to food and housing relief agencies. Regional Victorians are worse-off than their metro counterparts as the cost of living rises.

Data released recently by Lifeline reveals that around 500 calls each day are related to financial management, employment, or housing insecurity. Of this number, around 50 are focused on concerns related to homelessness.
