Council Facilities Open for Bookings

Dec 4, 2020 | News | 0

The Melbourne Chamber Orchestra will be performing in the Town Hall on 12 December

With the significant easing of COVID restrictions, Council will be making its facilities available for booking community events, meetings and activities, in line with State Government advice.

Additional signage and cleaning will be in place. Council has developed a Risk Management Plan to ensure these facilities can be opened safely and all users of facilities will be asked to play their part in helping the community to stay safe.

From Monday 7 December 2020, the following venues in Daylesford will be available for bookings:
• Daylesford Town Hall
• Daylesford Senior Citizens Hall
• Daylesford Victoria Park

Council’s Customer Service team are available to take bookings on 5348 2306.

Hire applications, with fees and conditions, can be downloaded from Council’s website here: https://www.hepburn.vic.gov.au/forms-fees/
