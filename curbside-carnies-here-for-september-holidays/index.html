Curbside Carnies Here for September Holidays

Aug 20, 2021 | Uncategorized, Living well | 0

Curbside Carnies is an innovative new performing arts project, devised and created during lockdown to bring some much-needed fun back to the Lockdown Capital of Australia.

Curbside Carnies is a circus for a COVID world.  It’s contactless! It’s distanced! And it’s sanitised!

The Carnies roll a custom-built roving stage to various locations to perform circus shows throughout the day. Audiences will be delighted by a variety of circus acts from neighbourhood performers – including hula hooping, juggling, acrobatics, unicycle and fire performance. The show includes interactive elements with group play time  and circus play space at end. The show is suitable for kids of all ages.

In the September School Holidays, Curbside Carnies are coming to the ARC. This event is sponsored by  by VicHealth and Daylesford Neighbourhood Centre. This is a free event but to comply with contact tracing rules tickets are required for each person who will be attending (ie: adults/carers/grandparents etc).

Date: September 23rd
Time: 2:00pm – 4:00pm
Location: Daylesford Arts, Recreation and Cultural Centre. 39 Smith Street, Daylesford, Victoria 3460
Cost: This event is free, but is a ticketed event. To comply with contact tracing requirements, please book a ticket for each person attending including parents and odhter accompanying persons.
Tickets: Get your tickets here: www.trybooking.com/BTNXE
