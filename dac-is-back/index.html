Daylesford Arts Collective – We are Back!

Jun 3, 2022 | Uncategorized, News | 0

D.A.C members at an early meeting: Front row (L to R): Vanessa Robertson, Selena Smith, Sandy Angliss Back row: Pam Gleeson, Paul Carney, Kim Johnson, Giorgio Buscema and Vanessa Craven (Absent members: Elizabeth De Koke and Mifany Reese).

The Daylesford Art Collective (DAC) is ready and raring to go with another popular ‘Pop up’ event of lovely art work by Daylesford residents. New artists will be displaying their work and (weather permitting) the event will be serenaded by Daylesford’s very own Lake Mist.

This ‘Pop up Art Show’ will be held over the Queen’s Birthday Weekend, June 11 to 13, between the hours of 10 am and 4 pm. The venue is No. 94 Vincent Street, adjacent to the Daylesford Information Centre. DAC is grateful to the Shire for providing this space free of charge. Without such support it would not be possible to put on this event.

We hope that one day it will be possible for DAC to operate in a space of its own in Daylesford. This would mutually benefit the community and visitors alike by a strong ‘Arts presence’ in the Shire which is currently lacking. In the mean time keep, tuned in for information about future ‘pop ups’.

Art works on display will be available for purchase.

If anyone is interested in finding out more about DAC, please contact Selena Smith at selenasmithartist@gmail.com or on 0407 290 742.
