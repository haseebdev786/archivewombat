Daylesford Conversation Online: The Energy Transition

May 8, 2026 | News | 0

The Daylesford Conversation on April 16th featured a lively discussion on the transition from fossil fuels to clean energy with Professor Bruce Mountain from Victoria University and Taryn Lane from Hepburn Energy facilitated by Robyn Rogers.

The Conversation covered a range of topics from transmission lines to roof top solar and the challenges of maintaining community support for the transition. The Conversation was recorded by Hepburn Radio. Click the link below to listen to the podcast.

Household electrification is a key strategy in the transition and the Wombat Post and Rotary will hold the next Conversation on ‘Electrifying Your Life on Thursday the 21st of May at the Community Bank Connections Room at 113 Main Road Hepburn Springs from 6-8.

Join us for the Conversation followed by a glass of wine and nibbles.
