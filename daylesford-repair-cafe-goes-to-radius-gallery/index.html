Daylesford Repair Café Goes to Radius Gallery

Apr 19, 2024 | News, Uncategorized | 0

Nikki Marshall

Radius Gallery in Hepburn Springs is the venue for April’s Repair Café.

While you get your items fixed you can enjoy the artwork around the gallery – quality artwork by locals living within a radius of Hepburn Springs.

There’ll be the usual range of repairs to household and garden items, as well as knife and tool sharpening, clothes repairs and bike maintenance.

And, of course, you can enjoy a cuppa, cake, and other treats at our regular café.

Our Repair Café is an excellent way to reduce waste to landfill, to maintain your household and garden items in good working order, to find new ways to keep wearing your favourite clothes, and also to extend the life of those precious and sentimental items in your care.

Come and join us this coming Sunday, April 21st, from 1pm to 4pm.

Date: Sunday April 21st

Time: 1:00pm to 4:00pm

Venue: Radius Art Gallery, 76 Main Road, Hepburn Springs
