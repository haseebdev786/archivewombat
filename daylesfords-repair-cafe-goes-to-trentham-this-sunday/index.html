Daylesford’s Repair Cafe Goes to Trentham this Sunday

May 17, 2024 | News | 0

Nikki Marshall

The Repair Café will join the Trentham Fashion Swap and Trentham Sustainability Group for the May fixer up.

In addition to the usual range of fixers and supporters who can mend bikes, household items, sharpen knives and tools and repair clothes,  you can re-purpose your favourite clothes for a longer life with the Trentham Fashion Swap, an initiative of the Trentham Sustainability Group.

When: Sunday May 19th, 1pm to 4pm
Where: Trentham Neighbourhood Centre, 56 High St, Trentham
