Developing a Masterplan for Central Springs Reserve

Apr 2, 2021 | News | 0

Council is looking for great ideas from the community to help revitalise the Central Springs Reserve, located just a short stroll from Lake Daylesford. The picturesque reserve has a grassy area and three mineral water pumps.

The reserve is located in the Wombat Creek valley, southwest of Lake Daylesford, downstream from the lake wall. A tree-damaged bandstand at the reserve was demolished in 2020. The provision of shelter at the reserve will be considered as part of the masterplan, following community feedback in 2019.
