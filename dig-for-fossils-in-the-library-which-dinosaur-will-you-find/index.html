Jun 25, 2021 | Uncategorized | 0

About this event

In large sandboxes, children will locate and identify fossils from some of the most iconic dinosaurs of the past.

Tyrannosaurus Rex, Stegosaurus, Triceratops and a few other surprises are all thrown in!
