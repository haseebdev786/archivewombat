Drill Bit Sharpening Workshop at Repair Cafe This Sunday

Sep 16, 2022 | Uncategorized, News | 0

A special workshop on sharpening drill bit led by Quentin Irvine will be a feature of the Repair Cafe this Sunday. (Photo: Mara Ripani)

Nikki Marshall

Join us at the Repair Cafe between 1pm and 4pm on Sunday, September 18th and see what our team of fixers are capable of repairing. We have a team of people who can sharpen knives, scissors and garden tools. We can mend clothes, cushions and soft toys. We can repair bicycles, prams and other wheeled items. And household appliances as well as electronics are also able to be fixed. Enjoy a cuppa, cake and conversation while you’re there. And we also have a ‘mending broken hearts table’ for those in need of a listening ear.

We’re always looking for people who can sew and repair clothes, so let us know if you’d like to join the repair team.

See you on Sunday for an afternoon of fixing.

Sharpening Drill Bits Workshop

Spring has sprung and it’s time to get all those jobs done around house and garden. So come along to the Repair Cafe this Sunday afternoon and learn how to sharpen drill bits so they don’t have to be thrown away or replaced. Quentin Irvine, local designer and builder, will show you how.

When: Sunday September 18th, 1.30-3.30pm
Where: Victoria Park Pavillion, Ballan Road, Daylesford
What to bring: Bring your drill and bits plus other tools in need of sharpening. If possible bring an angle grinder and safety glasses.
Cost:  Donation

Further information:

Contact Nikki Marshall (0432 232 073) or Danny Kinnear (0488 604 231) for more information.   Or check out the Daylesford Repair Cafe facebook page https://www.facebook.com/daylesfordrepaircafe/
