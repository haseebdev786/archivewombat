ELSIE KING

Apr 20, 2023 | Deaths | 0

12.10.1931 – 16.04.2023.
Loved daughter of Mary and William (both deceased).
Adored sister to Frank (dec), Lois (dec) and Alex (dec).
Treasured aunty, great aunty and great great aunty to many.
Family and friends are invited to the funeral service for Elsie to be held at the Verey Chapel, 43A Millar Street, Daylesford, on Friday 21st April 2023, commencing at 11:30am.
Following the service, we will cortege to the Daylesford Cemetery to lay Elsie to rest.
