Games at the Neighbourhood Centre

Apr 14, 2022 | News | 0

Lifesize Games

School holidays are here and if you’re looking for something to do outdoors in this glorious sunshine that won’t cost you anything why not come and have a try at the Life-size games! DNC currently have Chess and Jenna ready to go. Why not grab some friends, sit outside, enjoy a picnic and have some fun. Call to book a time on 5348 3569.

Free Court Hire at the ARC

Want to grab some friends and go and shoot some hoops! DNC are offering free court hire at the ARC during business hours and will be worked around any ongoing bookings we may already have booked in. Bookings are therefore essential and are time limited to 1hr for each booking. Access will be gained by a keycode provided at the time of booking. Call us on 5348 3569 if you would like to book a court. To book a court, we will need your name, phone number, address & email address. An Information Sheet with access code and information about alarm will be emailed to you at the time of booking.

The Neighbourhood Centre offers a range of courses as well, including floristry, responsible serving, exercise, yoga and first aid. Details and enrolment options for courses can be found here.
