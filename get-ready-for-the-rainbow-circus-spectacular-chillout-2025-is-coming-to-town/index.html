Get Ready for the Rainbow Circus Spectacular – ChillOut 2025 is Coming to Town!

Feb 28, 2025 | Uncategorized | 0

The ChillOut Parade on Sunday March 9 will revive an old circus tradition in our town. This photo is from a Daylesford circus parade probably in the 1950s. this yeaar, the parade will be led by a mechanical elephant, a recent quirky acquisition by Amazing Mill Market director, Mark Ward. Thanks to Will Walton of Belle property for sending it to The Wombat Post. Back in the day, the Belle Property office in Vincent Street was a chemist.

​Prepare to be dazzled as the ChillOut Festival returns to Daylesford next weekend, from 6th to 10th March 2025. Celebrating its 28th year, Australia’s premier regional LGBTQIA+ pride event invites you to experience the “Rainbow Circus Spectacular,” a vibrant fusion of community spirit, entertainment, and country charm.​

Festival Theme: Rainbow Circus Spectacular

This year’s theme, “Rainbow Circus Spectacular,” encourages attendees to embrace their inner performer. Anticipate a kaleidoscope of colourful costumes, captivating performances, and a celebration of queer culture in the heart of regional Victoria.​

Festival Highlights

The 2025 ChillOut Festival offers a diverse lineup of events catering to all tastes. Here are some must-attend highlights:​

  • Opening Night Extravaganza: Kick off the festivities on Thursday, 6th March, with a spectacular showcase featuring local talents and surprise circus acts under the Big Top at Victoria Park.​

  • Rainbow Spectacular Concert: On Friday, 7th March, enjoy an evening of music and entertainment with performances by renowned artists featuring Marcia Hines setting the stage for a weekend of celebration.​

  • Pool Party: Make a splash on Saturday, 8th March, at the family-friendly Pool Party. With live DJs, a community barbecue, and plenty of fun, it’s a refreshing way to spend the afternoon at Daylesford Outdoor Pool. From 12 pm until 4:00 pm.​

  • Street Parade: The iconic Street Parade takes over Vincent Street on Sunday, 9th March from 11:00 am to 12:00pm. Experience a vibrant procession of floats, costumes, and an explosion of rainbow pride, showcasing the essence of ChillOut.​

  • Carnival Day: Also on Sunday, Victoria Park transforms into a hub of music, food, and fun. Enjoy performances by various artists, a dog show, kids’ activities, and a picnic atmosphere—it’s a day for everyone.​

  • Closing Night Party: Conclude the weekend with a grand finale under the Big Top. Dance to infectious beats and celebrate with an electric crowd on Sunday evening.​

Join the Celebration

Whether you’re a ChillOut regular or a first-time attendee, the 2025 festival promises an unforgettable experience filled with joy, inclusivity, and a touch of country flair. Dress up in style and be part of Australia’s largest and longest-running queer country pride festival.​

For the full program and ticket information, visit the official ChillOut Festival website: chilloutfestival.com.au

Come and celebrate our diversity and our pride in our community.
