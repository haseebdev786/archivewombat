Gillian Moffat (nee Vanzetta)

Aug 13, 2021 | Deaths | 0

Our beloved Gillian Moffat has passed away on 24.7.21, at the age of just 61.
It is with tremendous pain in our hearts that we must share this news with all who loved her.
She dearly loved her 3 daughters Tynnelle, Suzanne & Nicole.
And, she adored, loved & doted on her 7 grandchildren, Hayden, Ryan, Maddison, Tyler, Dakota-May, Ivy-Jae and Jordan.
Mum you will forever be loved, missed, & remembered until the end of days by all who knew you. May you fly high & know that you are loved beyond measure.
R.I.P our dear mother and grandmother.
