After many years of waiting, in June 2021 the Daylesford Museum Reserve Committee of Management (DMRCoM) was gratified to receive a grant of $338,000 from the Victorian Government through the Department of Environment, Land, Water and Planning (DELWP). The grant was for urgent replacement of the original galvanised iron and slate roofs of the 130-year old Museum heritage building, formerly known as the School of Mines.
