Happy Wattle Day! Photo of the Week

Sep 6, 2024 | Features | 0

Wattles bordering a local garden dam on September 1, Wattle Day. Note the park bench beneath the tree on the left. (Photo: Tim Bach)

September 1 is celebrated as Wattle Day in Australia. Our gardens and forests are awash with the quintessential Australian bloom of wattles in spring.

This photo of a dam bordered by wattles in full bloom was taken on September 1. Happy Wattle Day!

Sadly, the storms earlier this week on the night of September 1, caused damage to many trees in our area including the trees in this photo. The photo below was taken on September 2. Sad Wattle Day!

 

 

Our community is full of real photographers and budding photographers and a lot of people who aren’t even photographers but get some great photos on their mobile phones. Share your best photos with The Wombat Post community. Send them in!
