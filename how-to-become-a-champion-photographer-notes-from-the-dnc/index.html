How to Become a Champion Photographer – Notes From the DNC

Feb 14, 2025 | News | 0

There are two important courses starting shortly at the DNC – one on smartphome photography and another on safe food handling. For queries about these or any other matters, contact Daylesford Neighbourhood Centre on Phone: 03 5348 3569 or Email: admin@dncentre.org.au

Smartphone Photography

Are you less than happy with some of the photos you take with your phone?  Do you envy those of your friends who seem to always take beautiful scenes and portraits?  Relax!  We have the answer!

DNC are excited to announce a new course for 2025.   We are offering a “Smartphone Photography” course, commencing in March.

This course, a series of workshops, will cover some of the key design principles behind composition and colour, as well as exploring mobile editing apps to help take your photos to the next level.  There are 3 workshops/sessions scheduled, and you can do one, two or all three sessions.  More information on the individual sessions can be found on our website https://www.dncentre.org.au/courses

Participants will require a smart phone, a pen and their phone charger.  You are also welcome to bring lunch and dress appropriately for a 30-minute walk in the area around the Neighbourhood Centre.  

Kirsty Fox is our Tutor.  She is a Photographer, over-thinker and lifelong learner. She has worked in education and in training staff and patrons on library management software.  Kirsty will supply a zine-style booklet that will cover key points from the course content, space for notes as well as a list of further resources.

Dates: Wednesdays 5, 12, 19 March 2025
Time: 10:30 am – 1:30 pm
Cost: All 3 workshops $120.00 (Concession $110.00) Individual workshops each $45.00 (Concession $35.00)
Bookings:  https://socialplanet.com.au/activity/view?id=31686

Food Handlers Course

This course is a MUST-DO for anyone working in the food industry.  Scheduled for 20th February 2025, this course relies on minimum attendances.  If you are considering doing this course, don’t hesitate – book in now!   The course will be held only if there are sufficient numbers..

Date: Thursday, February 20, 2025
Information and Bookings: https://www.dncentre.org.au/courses .
