Jean Margaret Howard (nee Towers)

Feb 24, 2026 | Deaths | 0

22.03.1929 – 17.02.2026

 

Jean passed away peacefully at Hailey House in Ballarat surrounded by her family.

 Loved wife of Tom (dec), loved and loving mother of Suzanne, Terry and Amanda, loved mother-in-law of David, Ann and Geoff.

Many thanks to all those involved in the care of Jean.

Family and friends are invited to celebrate the life of Jean Howard to be held at Verey Chapel, 43a Millar Street, Daylesford on Thursday 26th February 2026 commencing at 2pm.

Following the service, we will cortege to the Daylesford Cemetery to lay Jean to rest.

 

For those unable to attend, the service will be live streamed via vereyfuneraldirectors.com.au
