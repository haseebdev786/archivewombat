Keep Your Kids Safe with a Car Restraint Safety Check – and an UNREASONABLY FUNNY Joke – Notes from the DNC

Nov 10, 2023 | News | 0

The DNC, through Neighbourhood Houses Vic have once again teamed up with the Safe Seats Safe Kids program.

Local parents and carers are invited to book in for a free child car restraint fitting or safety check on 17 November at DNC, 13 Camp St Daylesford!

Only a few appointments available, and all bookings are via the Safe Seats Safe Kids website.

This event is hosted by Kidsafe Victoria in partnership with Neighbourhood Houses Victoria, with support by the Victorian State Government.

DNC Joke of the Week

A man goes into a drug store and asks the pharmacist if he can give him something for the hiccups. The pharmacist promptly reaches out and slaps the man’s face.

“What did you do that for?” the man asks.

“Well, you don’t have the hiccups anymore do you?”

The man says, “No, but my wife out in the car still does!”
