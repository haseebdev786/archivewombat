Lights, Camera, Trivia! Support the Phoenix at the Rex!

Apr 18, 2025 | Uncategorized | 0

Love movies? Think you know your Tarantino from your Hitchcock? Then gather your friends and get ready for a night of fun, laughter, and friendly competition at the Movie Trivia Quiz Night, hosted by the Daylesford Cinema Group to raise funds for the much-anticipated Phoenix Cinema at the Rex.

Join us on Wednesday, May 7 at the Daylesford Bowls Club (8 Camp Street, Daylesford). Doors open at 6:00 PM and the trivia kicks off at 6:45 PM sharp.

Your glamorous host for the evening will be none other than Daylesford favourite Dolly Disco, who’s guaranteed to bring sparkle and sass to the night.

Form a team of 8 or 10. Tickets are $45 and include trivia quiz cards and generous platters of snacks for your table. Drinks will be available at bar prices.

All proceeds from the event will go toward fitting out the new Phoenix Cinema, set to reopen later this year – a major boost for arts and culture in our community.

To book your spot, visit Humanitix. For more information or special dietary needs, contact Ian McKnight on 0478 486 888.

So start brushing up on your movie knowledge and thinking of a name for your team – it’s going to be a blockbuster of a night!
