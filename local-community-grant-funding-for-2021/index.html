Local Community Grant Funding for 2021

Feb 5, 2021 | News | 0

Hepburn Shire Grant funding for community groups for 2021 is now available.

Community groups or organisations looking for funding for a new or one-off event or program can apply for Round 3 of Council’s Community Grants.

Categories include:

  1. Active and Engaged Communities (including Welcome to New Residents Sessions)
  2. Quality Community Infrastructure (including small equipment)
  3. Sustainable Environments
  4. Children’s Program (specifically for 8 – 12 years)

The guidelines are available on the Hepburn Shire Website.

Applications close on 1st March 2021.
