Margaret Hazler

Jul 23, 2025 | Deaths | 0

(nee Seamons)

13.06.1939 – 21.07.2025

Passed away peacefully at the Daylesford Hospital.Loving wife of Alan (dec).

Mother of Brian and grandmother of Tianna.

Loved daughter of Leslie and Kathleen Seamons (both dec)

Loving sister of Brian, Florence, Kathy (all dec), Irene, Doreen, Josie, Clare, Leonie and John.

FUNERAL DETAILS

Family and friends are invited to celebrate the life of Margaret Hazler at the Verey Chapel 43A Millar Street Daylesford on Friday 25th July, 2025 commencing at 11.00am. 

Following the wake at the Chapel, we will cortege to the Sandon Cemetery, 801 Creswick Newstead Road Sandon to lay Margaret to rest.
