UPDATE: Meeting to Establish a Hospital Campaign Committee

Jul 30, 2021 | Uncategorized, Living well, News | 0

Lesley Hewitt

A public meeting to establish a community based Campaign Committee to support a much needed upgrade to the Daylesford Hospital will now be held by Zoom because of COVID restrictions.

The Zoom meeting will be held on Wednesday, August 4 at 7:00pm. All members of the community are welcome.

For further information contact Cr Lesley Hewitt (lesley.hewitt55@gmail.com) or Kate Redwood (c.redwood@bigpond.com) by email. They are maintaining a list of people who have contacted them but would welcome further interest. The code for the Zoom meeting can be obtained from either Kate or Lesley.

Lesley Hewitt is a Councillor for Birch Ward.
