Men’s Group and More – Notes from the DNC

Sep 20, 2024 | News | 0

Men’s Group

A Men’s Group is starting at DNC in October. These sessions are designed for self-exploration, sharing and support.

  • Speak your truth.
  • Be listened to and be heard.
  • Support others to say what they need to say.
  • Explore challenges and life issues.
  • Enjoy the camaraderie in a safe and supportive environment.

The minimum age for attendees is 18. Enrolments are essential.

Dates: 10 & 24 Oct – 07 & 21 Nov – 05 Dec.
Cost: $50 covers cost of all 5 sessions
More information: If you would like to take part, contact Greg ‘Arjuna’ Govinda on 0466 339 287

French Alive!

Are you someone who learnt French years ago and would like to refresh your skills? Or maybe you are planning to travel to a French-speaking country and would like to enrich your experience? Or perhaps you’d simply like to refine your understanding of the French language?

This course offers a mix of conversational French and structured language lessons in a fun, relaxed and supportive environment.

We welcome Catherine LeRoux, our new French tutor.

Classes commence 7th October and bookings can be made by going to our website Courses page

DNC Joke of the Week

There was a small African tribe that had a terrible problem. Every morning a neighbouring herd of Wildebeests would stampede through the village, knocking down cooking pots, smashing water jugs and trampling the vegetable gardens.

The Chief had tried everything, with no success. He built fences, but the Gnus ran right through them. He tried having his men scare the herd away but they always came back. Finally, one morning the Chief woke up and didn’t hear the thunder of hooves. He went out, and the Gnu herd was nowhere to be seen. They were gone at last!

So, the Chief called all the people of the tribe together, and announced, “NO GNUS IS GOOD NEWS!”
