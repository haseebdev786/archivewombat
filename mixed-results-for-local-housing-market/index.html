Mixed Results for Local Housing Market

May 1, 2026 | Features, News | 0

Houses “for sale”  increased slightly  to 181 houses (from 178 in Dec)  in the Daylesford and surrounding areas (postcodes 3460 and 3461) with 104 for sale  in Daylesford.

Over the past two years the Victorian regional housing market has shown a strong recovery with a growth of six percent over 2025 as supply tightened and demand increased. Median house prices have risen to approximately $650,000.

Daylesford median house prices remain significantly higher than the regional average, but in contrast to regional trends, Daylesford median prices have remained flat at about $820,000. According to real estate.com, 77 houses were sold in Daylesford over the past 12 months and the average time on the market was 128 days.

Houses for sale over last year

Over the past four months, the number of houses for sale in the Daylesford increased slightly and total houses on the market was 16.8% greater than 12 months ago. 

From 01 Jan to 30 Apr period 42 houses were reported as sold with 50% (21) being in Daylesford. 

Houses Sold since 1/1/2026

17 of the 21 houses sold in Daylesford listed their sell prices. Of these 13 were under $1mill and 7 were under $700K. 

Of the 104 Daylesford properties 18 were listed at prices less that $700K. With 50 listed with asking prices greater that $1 mill. The Daylesford median price was $995K.

Houses by price range

Source – realestate.com 

Local agents indicate that although there is reasonable demand for properties  upto $1 mill, sellers above this value may be in for a long wait or need to reassess the expectations.

17% of houses (31) offered in the area are listed at prices under $700K. There were also 11 properties listed between $450K and $600K, which opens opportunities for new entrants into the market
