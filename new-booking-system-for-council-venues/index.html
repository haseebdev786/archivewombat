New Booking System for Council Venues

Jul 7, 2023 | News | 0

Daylesford Senior Citizens Centre. (Photo: Hepburn Shire Council)

Hepburn Shire has launched a new on-line booking system for Council venues. The new system will make booking a Council venue for an event much simpler and easier. The system will also be available outside of normal office hours.

The system will enable users to see images of the venue along with available dates and costs and should eliminate the problems ofÂ  accidental double bookings.

Some of the venues that will be available to book online are:

  • Daylesford Town Hall
  • Daylesford Senior Citizens
  • Hepburn Spring Mineral Spring Reserve
  • Lake Daylesford and Central Springs Mineral Springs Reserve
  • Lake Jubilee
  • Victoria Park Pavilion, Daylesford
  • Wombat Hill Gardens
  • Clunes community Centre
  • Creswick Hub Meeting room
  • Clunes Town Hall
  • Clunes Warehouse (Esmond Gallery)
  • Clunes Warehouse (Ulumbarra Room)
  • Creswick Town Hall
  • Doug Lindsay Reserve Pavilion
  • Park Lake, Creswick

Other venues will be added to the new booking system in the future.Â  For venues not currently listed in the booking system, the community can continue to call customer service staff for assistance.
