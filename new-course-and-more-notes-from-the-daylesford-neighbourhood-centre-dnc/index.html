Nov 1, 2024 | News | 0

Are you someone who learnt French years ago and would like to refresh your skills? Or perhaps you’d simply like to refine your understanding of the French language? DNC has a new course starting on 18th November! Small class; 5 sessions only; held Mondays 1.30pm

Book here

 

Help your Neighbourhood Centre

DNC has recently signed up to be a Donation Partner with the Container Deposit Scheme!

If you have bottles and containers that are eligible for a refund, DNC would like to work with you and would be grateful for your 10c donations.

We have recently signed up as a Donation Partner with the Container Deposit Scheme.  This me

ans, we can accept donations of refunds for anyone who wishes to donate to a charity.  Next time you have a few bottles to take to the recycle station, if you would like to donate your refund to a local group, you can now select DNC as the recipient.

To send your donation through to DNC:

  1. Download the CDS VicWest App from the app store
  2. Search “Charities” in the Searchbox and select DNC
  3. Once on the DNC page, scroll down and click SET AS PAYOUT button!
  4. At the recycle station, scan your barcode at the scanner, pop your bottles in and that’s it!

Simple!

If you are part of a local group and would like to support DNC with your refunds, we would be very grateful for any donations and happy to chat to you further if you would like to do something officially with your group.   We can help with flyers etc.  Just call us and we’d be happy to work with you!
