New Museum Exhibition Celebrates Wombat Hill Botanic Gardens

Aug 2, 2024 | Uncategorized, Living well | 0

Snow on Wombat Hill from the Ken Duxbury postcard collection. Helen Page OAM will speak about the collection on August 4.

The Daylesford Museum is supporting the Friends of the Wombat Hill Botanical Gardens with a new exhibition, Wombat Hill Botanic Gardens, as part of the  Winter in the Gardens festival this weekend.

The exhibition will be open both Saturday and Sunday, August 3 and 4, from 11:00 am to 3:00 pm and will continue on Saturdays through August.

Highlights of the exhibit include historic plans and images combined with several works of art. A series of 1960s images of snow covered Daylesford, by former resident Peter Lane, is a highlight of the exhibition. Rare film footage of 1915 events in the area that includes a gathering at Wombat Hill will also feature in the exhibit.

This exhibition is supported by a Hepburn Shire Community Grant, the Community Bank Daylesford District and the Rosalind Pyers Exhibition Fund. Local U3A members and museum volunteers will staff the exhibition over the opening weekend.
