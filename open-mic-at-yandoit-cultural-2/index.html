Open Mic at Yandoit Cultural

May 10, 2024 | Uncategorized, Living well | 0

Nikki Marshall

Join us at Yandoit Cultural this Saturday afternoon, May 11th from 3pm-5pm for an afternoon of stories, song, music, poetry and history shared by locals.

Anyone and everyone can have their 5-10 minutes. You’ll hear a variety of performances and discover the many talents within and around our neighbourhood. And afterwards enjoy a cuppa and cake with other locals and Open Mic contributors.

When: Saturday May 11, 3PM TO 5PM
Where: Yandoit Cultural, Uniting Church Rd, off High St, Yandoit
Tickets: Entry by donation.
More Information: Contact Katy on 0430 530 868
