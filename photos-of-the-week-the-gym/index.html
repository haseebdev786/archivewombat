Health and Wellness in East Street: Life at the Gym

Apr 17, 2026 | Features, Living well | 0

The East St business precinct has become something of a Mecca for fitness and strength work. There are now five fitness businesses operating in the East Street and Mink Street area. They include the Springs Health and Fitness, Run with Me, Strong Space, Daylesford Cross Fit and Integrate Fitness.

Photos: Julie Higgs
