Planning Applications – New Daylesford Child Care Centre Proposal

Nov 3, 2023 | News | 0

30 Hospital St Daylesford is the site of a proposed 100 place child care facility. (Image: Goole Street View)

A planning application (PLN23 0027) for a childcare centre with 100 places in Hospital St has been advertised.

The  application for  30 Hospital St submitted on behalf of local developer Eddy Comelli is seeking a 2 lot subdivision of an existing 2 acre (8094 sq mtr) property. The site is zoned Neighbourhood Residential and  is adjacent to Doctor’s Gully. A number of properties on Vincent Street south of Hospital Street back onto the property.

The proposed childcare (Lot 1) is  2296 m2 or 28% of the existing land. The remainder of the block (Lot 2) is 5799 m2 or 72% of the block. Although no usage has been nominated for Lot 2 the application notes that it allows for numerous design options including future subdivision.

The application for the Childcare centre consists of a single storey facility for up to 100 children (0 – 5yrs plus a “kinder” area). The facility is to have 28 car spaces (increased from 22 originally nominated).

When contacted by The Wombat Post, Mr Commelli said that there was a definite need for additional child care facilities in Daylesford and he thought that the development would be of benefit to the community.

Public submission relating to this application close 20 November
