PLUCK! Plucked-String Music From Around the World

Dec 12, 2025 | Uncategorized, Living well | 0

Yandoit Cultural presents PLUCK! Enjoy an afternoon of plucked-string music from around the world.

Join PLUCK! as they celebrate the launch of their debut album, “Visitors”, which pays tribute to the diverse musical traditions that inspire them. Their show will feature tunes from their recent recordings, alongside old favourites, traversing West African, Brazilian, Flamenco, bluegrass, jazz and blues roots.

What began as three guitarist-friends sharing with each other the music that inspires them, grew into a joyous exploration of the wonderfully varied music for plucked strings from around the globe. Hailing from diverse musical backgrounds, their members unite around a shared love of new horizons, improvised mischief and the well placed musical dad-joke.

Expect the unexpected – anything from choro to Czardas de Monti, from bluegrass to bulerías.

Noah James on guitars, mandolin and vocals; Ryan Griffith on Guitars, bass and vocals; and Luke Andreas on Guitars, percussion and vocals are guests in the music they explore. Their mission: to travel with humility and playfulness as far as their armada of pluckable instruments will carry them, through the myriad sonic worlds born of lumps of wood and bits of string.

Their show will feature tunes from their recent recordings alongside old favourites, traversing West African, Brazilian, Flamenco, bluegrass, jazz and blues roots.

Date: Sunday, 14th December 2025
Time: 4:30 pm
Venue: Yandoit Cultural, Uniting Church Rd, off High St, Yandoit
Bookings: Humanitix : Pluck! “Visitors” Album Launch

Two sets with a brief intermission. Light refreshments provided. BYO welcome.
