Prisoner #1056 – The Second Thursday Book Club Review

Jul 21, 2023 | Living well | 0

L to R: Shanthi Shanthikumar, Malini Shathikumar and Roy Ratnavel at the book launch in Melbourne. (Photo supplied)

With Max Primmer and Lesley Hewitt

In this month’sh edition of the Second Thursday Book Club on Hepburn Community Radio, Max and Lesley interview Daylesford local Malini Shanthikumar about the book “Prisoner #1056” written by Roy Ratnavel. In the book, Roy tells the story of his imprisonment in Sri Lanka as a young Tamil man, his emigration to Canada and his rise to prominence as a finance executive in the asset management industry.

You can listen to the interview here, https://soundcloud.com/hepburn-community-radio/second-thursday-book-club
