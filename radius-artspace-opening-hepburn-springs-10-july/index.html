Radius ArtSpace Opening | Hepburn Springs (10 July)

Jul 9, 2021 | Uncategorized | 0

Exciting new local Art-space, Radius is opening up to the public.

Located in Hepburn Springs, this vibrant new local Arts-space has a focus to local artists within a 50-kilometre radius. Radius co-director & curator Kim Percy has put together an opening exhibition highlighting 10 local artists. Artists utilise a mix of creative ideas, skilfully curated to provide a highly immersive visual experience & celebrate local creative talent.

“Our story begins with an idea that our community and region have an integrative creative soul”, says Kim.

“We want to showcase our local making for all to see, hear, taste and touch.”

“Our immersive Radius journey and shared relationship begins at home,” Kim adds.

Local creatives include an eclectic mix of established and emerging local artists including the multi-discipline artist, Jo Mott, painter Gav Barbey, renowned Petrus Spronk and highly talented ceramist Mel Ogden and Minna Graham.

All works are available for sale.

All artist details and items pricing can be found on Radius website.

Visit Radius website for event details.

Come along to be part of the Radius journey, a local vision of creativity and life.
