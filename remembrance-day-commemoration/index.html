Remembrance Day Commemorations

Nov 8, 2024 | Features | 0

Every year at the 11th hour of the 11th day of the 11th month, millions around the world pause in silence to remember the sacrifices many have made in the service of their country.

Pvt. Sebastian Tatti

Amongst those who served from the Daylesford area is Private Sebastian Tatti.

Private Tatti was born in Daylesford in 1883 where he spent his childhood and attended the Daylesford State School. He enslisted in the Australian Infantry in September 1914 at Blackboy Hill in Western Australia where he was probably working as a miner.

Private Tatti served in the 12th Infantry Battalion which left for Europe from Fremantle in November 1914 on the HMAT Medic. He was a medic for the battalion.

Pvt. Frank Tatti

Private Tatti was killed in action on the 27th of March 1917 in France at Bullecourt. He is buried in the Vault Hill Cemetery.

His brothers Frank and Henry also served in the AIF. Frank was killed in action in Belgium in September 1917.

The Tatti brothers service to Australia is commemorated on the Australian War Memorial Roll of Honour, Boulder Roll of Honor and the Daylesford War Memorial.

There will be local commemorations for the Tatti brothers and all those others from around the Shire who served on Monday morning, November 11th:

Daylesford: Remembrance Service and wreath laying at the Daylesford Cenotaph, 10:45am arrival for 11:00am start.

Creswick: Remembrance Service and wreath laying at the Creswick Cenotaph, 10:30 arrival for a 10:45am start.

Kingston: Remembrance Service and wreath laying at Kingston Cenotaph, corner Victoria Street and Kingston Avenue, 10:30am arrival for 10:45am start

Clunes: Remembrance Service and wreath laying at Cenotaph, cnr Fraser and Service Streets, Clunes, 10:50am start

Trentham: Remembrance Service and wreath laying at the Trentham Cenotaph, 10:45am
