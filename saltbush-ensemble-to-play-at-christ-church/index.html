Saltbush Ensemble to Play at Christ Church

Sep 22, 2023 | Features | 0

The highly successful early music Saltbush Ensemble will play at Christchurch on the 30th of September.

Formed in 2017 in Melbourne, long time musical collaborators and friends Reidun Turner and Ryan Williams (pictured) reunited after many years abroad, forming the Saltbush Ensemble to showcase the range and depth of Early Music in Australia.

Saltbush are returning to Daylesford after their successful concert last year in our first concert series.

The Saltbush Ensemble collaborate with various Early Music artists developing unique and varied programs for an Australian audience and responding to an increasing insatiable thirst for historical or Early Music.

The name of the ensemble was inspired by the native Australian Saltbush plant which grows in many varied environments and is particularly renowned for its rejuvenating properties in salt ravaged landscapes. Like a Saltbush, the rekindling of Early Music in Australia is rejuvenating the traditional classical music scene bringing new perspectives and repertoire into the fold.

The concert will commence at 2.30pm at Christ Church on Saturday 30 September, 54 Central Springs Road, Daylesford. Visit the Christ Church Concerts Facebook
