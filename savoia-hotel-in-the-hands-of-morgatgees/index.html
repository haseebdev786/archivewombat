Savoia Hotel in the Hands of Mortgagees

May 26, 2023 | News | 0

A notice has been posted on the Historic Savoia Hotel in Main Road, Hepburn, stating that the Mortgagee has taken possession of the land.

Earlier this month, a notice had been posted on the doors saying that the Savoia was closing for three weeks for renovations and an update to their menu.

The Hotel reopened after a lengthy closure in September 2022 and its return was warmly welcomed by the local community.

The hotel was purchased about two years ago by Paul Morwood, an ex-AFL footballer who played for South Melbourne, St.Kilda and Collingwood in the 1980s.
