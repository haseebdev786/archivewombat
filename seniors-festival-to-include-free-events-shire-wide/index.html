Seniors Festival to Include Free Events Shire Wide

Sep 16, 2022 | Uncategorized, Living well | 0

The 40th annual Seniors Festival will feature a number of free concerts and events and getting there will be easier thanks to free public transport for seniors during the Festival.

Mayor, Cr Tim Drylie, said that in line with Hepburn Shire’s  ‘No Barrier’ Positive Ageing Strategy, Council was working with the local community to increase access to events and address social isolation.

“There are currently over 7,000 residents in Hepburn Shire over the age of 55, which is 46% of the Shire’s population. The the State average is 28%. This makes the Seniors Festival even more important to us.”

The 2022 Victorian Seniors Festival will feature live and online events, including concerts, dances, sport, the arts, history talks and tour, with events across the State supported by the Victorian Government.  The events are designed to empower seniors to connect, socialise and further their wellbeing.

This year’s theme, ‘Takin’ it easy’, focuses on presenting accessible and comfortable spaces, both live and online, to ensure our seniors feel relaxed to participate and ease back into events.

Building on the success of the previous Victorian Seniors Festivals, this year’s events will include online and radio programs, as well as First Nations performances and entertainment from multicultural artists.

The Country Concert series will take the festival to 26 regional towns and the Aged Care Tour will entertain aged care residents with music and magicians from September to December.

To ensure older Victorians can make the most of the Festival, Victorian Seniors Card holders can access free travel on public transport (including V/Line) services from October 2 to 9. Even though the events listed below occur outside of those dates, free transport will be available for all events. Details will be provided when you make a booking.

For V/Line trips that require a reservation, bookings open on Tuesday 6 September. More information here: seniorsonline.vic.gov.au/free-travel

Information programs can be found at Coles stores, local councils, libraries and community organisations.

For more information about the Victorian Seniors Festival visit www.seniorsonline.vic.gov.au/festival or call 1300 797 210.
