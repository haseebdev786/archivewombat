Shire Runs Drop In Sessions for Those Affected By the Storm

Jul 30, 2021 | News | 0

Somehow this Goldfields Track post survived trees crashing either side of it. (Photo: Bill Casey.)

If you were affected by the recent storm event in the Hepburn Shire, Council is inviting you to come along to a Storm Recovery Community Drop-in Session.

The sessions are an opportunity for residents and business operators to talk to staff from relief and recovery agencies, along with Council staff from teams including Emergency Management, Infrastructure, Building, Planning, Environmental Health, Economic Development and Community Care. Farmers and Producers are also encouraged to attend, Agriculture Victoria will be there.

There will be information available on support for storm recovery, and an opportunity to speak with experts in an informal setting.

Council invites you to attend a session on Monday 9 August at the Trentham Sportsground Pavilion on Falls Road, Trentham. There will be five sessions available:
