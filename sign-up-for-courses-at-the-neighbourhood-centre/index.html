Sign Up for Courses at the Neighbourhood Centre

Oct 8, 2021 | News | 0

As the Neighbourhood Centre navigates its way through to the new COVID world, they are gearing up to run as many classes as they can in their shortened Term 4. The intention is to catch up with many people in coming weeks as they reschedule their courses to November dates. Meanwhile, they are looking for expressions of interest for courses like Auslan, First Aid and Basic Computer Skills (details below). Some dates are still being finalised and will be advertised when arrangements are made.

Aromatherapy Course

Intro to Aromatherapy is locked in and commencing Mon 8th November! This course will introduce you to the history of aromatherapy and how essential oils affect and influence the human mind and body. The Course will look at how essential oils are distilled; how they work and the different ways in which to apply essential oils safely. Start to build your knowledge of aromatherapy to incorporate into your massage practise or for personal use. Several essential oils will be studied over the 6 weeks and some handy recipes and uses of essential oils will be shared.

Tai Chi Course

Wu Style Tai Chi, a gentle and slow, but exacting form of exercise based on an ancient Chinese tradition. It is suitable for all age groups and helps support flexibility, concentration, memory and balance, as well as inner peace. For anyone looking at trying Tai Chi, it will be moving to a weekly format for Term 4. Classes run for 60mins and commence at 10.30am. $15 per session. No previous experience required. Please get in touch with Gudrun, if you would like to take part 0490 053 521

The DNC is doing a call-out for anyone interested in doing a Basic Computer Skills Course. It is envisaged this course will run through Term 4, after Nov 5 – if enough people register interest. The course will be aimed at participants with very little knowledge of computers. Topics covered will include MS Office suite of products, Email, Search Online, Email set up, Social Media etc. It will be a 5 week course, occurring one day a week and running for 4 hours each week. Course Tutor, Mike Soccio, will guide you through the computer basics required for every day functionality and communications. To Register your interest in this course, email DNC at admin@dncentre.org.au
