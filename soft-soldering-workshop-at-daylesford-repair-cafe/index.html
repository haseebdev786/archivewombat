Soft Soldering Workshop at Daylesford Repair Café

Aug 15, 2025 | Uncategorized | 0

The August Repair Café we will include a Workshop on the application of “Soft Soldering” and its many uses. Some of the uses include making and repairing Jewellery, assembling circuit boards, repairing electronic devices, securing electrical components and joining copper pipes and fittings. The Workshop will be run by Susan and Julian, our expert Fixers in this area. Come along and see what can be achieved.

In addition to the Workshop there’ll be the usual repair stations: mechanical, knife and tool sharpening, electrical, clothes, household items and more. We’ll have the usual cuppa, cake and conversation at our Café.

We had a successful Darning Workshop in July, with many new skills learned and good conversation held around the table, among cups of tea/coffee and a bite to eat from the delicious selection of sweet and savoury afternoon tea.

When: Sunday August 17th, 1.00-4.00pm
Where: Victoria Park Pavillion, Daylesford-Ballan Rd.

Look out for the flags at the entrance of Victoria Park.
