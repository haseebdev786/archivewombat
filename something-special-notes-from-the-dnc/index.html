May 16, 2025 | News | 0

Become a DNC Member – Become a Part of Something Special

At the Daylesford Neighbourhood Centre, community is at the heart of everything we do. And we’d love for you to be part of it.

Whether you’re a familiar face or someone we’re yet to meet, we warmly invite you to become a member. It’s a simple, meaningful way to show your support for the values we share:  inclusion, connection and opportunities for everyone.

By becoming a member, you’re helping to create a place where everyone feels welcome and heard.  You’ll be supporting our Community Access and Participation Policy and you’ll have the chance to be involved in our Annual General Meetings while staying in touch with everything happening at DNC.  Most of all, you’ll be part of a growing network of good people who believe in the power of community.

Here’s how to join:

  • Complete the  DNC Membership Form and return it to us in person at DNC (13 Camp St, Daylesford) or by email at admin@dncentre.org.au
  •  Take a look at our DNC Annual Report to see what we’ve been up to
  • Come along to our AGM on Tuesday 27 May at 6pm @ DNC and start thinking about where we’re     headed next!

We’d love to have you on the team, to keep building something wonderful together.
