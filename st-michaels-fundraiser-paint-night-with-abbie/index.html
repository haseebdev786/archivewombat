St. Michaels Fundraiser: Paint Night with Abbie

May 14, 2021 | News | 0

St. Michaels Primary School is holding a fundraiser on June 18th to upgrade of their current presentation room with new tables and chairs and a fresh paint.

‘Paint Night with Abbie’ is hands-on painting workshop open to the public. The community is invited to come along and enjoy a fun night with friends and support the school. Enjoy a BYO wine, nibbles and painting as Abbie May guides you through with comprehensive step by step instruction with plenty of room for your creative side to run wild. By the end of the night, you will be taking home your very own one of a kind piece of artwork and a night to remember.

A silent auction with loads of great donates from local business, door prizes and games throughout the evening. The event is limited to 30 people. Cost is $40 per person with all the necessary equipment provided. The only thing you’ll need to bring is some drinks and nibbles and your imagination.

This is the first Paint Night with Abbie and, if successful, more sessions will run in the future to keep the fundraising going for the school.

For bookings contact Leah at St. Michaels Primary School 5348 1261.
