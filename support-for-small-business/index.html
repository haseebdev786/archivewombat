Support for Small Business

Feb 10, 2023 | News | 0

Hepburn Shire Council, in partnership with Business Victoria, is providing free face-to-face workshops and online webinars for small business operators. The workshops are being facilitated by the Monarch Institute and are being held in Daylesford and Creswick.

The workshops provide an opportunity for business operators to participate in person and network with other small business owners.

A host of free online webinars for businesses are also available. These are being offered across multiple days and times and provide an opportunity to enhance social media skills and strategies.

Mayor, Cr Brian Hood, said it is great to see in-person workshops once again being offered to support business owners and operators across the Shire.
