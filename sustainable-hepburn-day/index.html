Sustainable Hepburn Day

Feb 9, 2024 | News | 0

The first Sustainable Hepburn Day held in Trentham last year was an informative and fun day for the community. A follow-up event is planned this year at Victoria Park on March 16.

The day will feature workshops and interactive displays will celebrate all many of the sustainability initiatives in Hepburn Shire to help you with your own sustainability journey at home and at work. Live music, games, food and drink will ensure that there is something for everyone in the family.

A highlight of the day will be the Circular Fashion precinct with many chances to swap clothes, pick up amazing op shop bargains and craft your own crazy costumes from waste for our Circular Catwalk.

Stallholders who will be there include:

  • Black Snake Productions
  • Repair Café
  • WrongTown Boutique
  • Uparell
  • Trentham Sustainability Group
  • Enviroshop
  • Hepburn Gardens for Wildlife
  • Wombat Native Plants
  • Hepburn Wildlife Shelter
  • Wombat Forestcare
  • Hepburn Distillery
  • Transition Creswick

Some of the activities and features include:

  • Welcome to Country
  • Sustainable Fashion Catwalk
  • Bands: Elio and the Shinkicks; The Hobyahs
  • Costume Crafting
  • Love your Compost
  • Wildlife display
  • Electrify your home
  • Workshops
  • Crazy bicycles
  • Local food and drink stalls
  • Treasure Hunt
  • Lawn Games
  • Hepburn Shire Council Information Stalls
  • Prizes
  • EV Car Display

The event is supported by Hepburn Shire Council and the Victorian Government.

This article is based on a media release from Hepburn Shire Council.
