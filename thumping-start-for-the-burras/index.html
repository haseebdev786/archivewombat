Thumping Start for the Burras

Apr 14, 2022 | News, Sport and recreation | 0

Six players made their debut with Hepburn seniors on the weekend. Left to Right: Mitch Banner (Senior Coach), Nash Robinson, Cooper Bath, Billy Pedretti, Brock Noble and Sean Tighe (Assistant Coach). (Photo: Katherine Cooper)

Hepburn Burras Seniors got their season off to a thumping start with a 31.20-206 win over Ballan (1.3-9) at Laurie Sullivan Oval on Saturday. In his first game with the Burras, playing coach, Mitch Banner, was Best on Ground with 10 goals. Cooper Bath kicked five and Jackson Hogan and Brayden Yanner kicked three each. The reserves and junior sides were undefeated on the day. Under 12s won 3.2-20 to 1.7-13. Under 15s tied 6.4-40 each. Under 18s won 17.17-119 to 1.2-8 and the Reserves won 21.20-146 to 1.2-8.
