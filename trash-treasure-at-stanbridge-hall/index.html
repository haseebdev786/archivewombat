Trash & Treasure at Stanbridge Hall

Sep 30, 2022 | News, Uncategorized | 0

Are you thinking of having a garage sale but don’t have all that much stuff to sell? Or don’t want people sorting through your stuff at home?

Why not get a trestle table at Stanbridge Hall on October 29? You can reserve a trestle table for $50 and take advantage of what promises to be a good crowd of potential buyers.

The Trash & Treasure at Stanbridge Hall is the first initiative in a Stanbridge Hall Renovation Fund campaign to continue the refurbishment of the Hall which has already started. Already we have introduced reverse cycle heating and cooling, new lighting and restored the natural beauty of the now highly polished floor. There is more to do including repainting, upgrading kitchen facilities and exterior works including replacement of some windows in line with the building’s heritage listing. Our Renovation Fund will be applied to these works.

The Hall, built in 1857 and funded by William Stanbridge, commenced as a girls school and place of worship.

Stanbridge Hall is located at 54 Central Springs Road, Daylesford. For more information, or to reserve a table, phone Anne on 0408 038 717. And if you’re in the market for a bargain, put the date in your diary.
