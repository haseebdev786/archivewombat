Wack a Weed , Save a Tree at Cornish Hill

Mar 18, 2022 | News, Uncategorized | 0

Our community relies heavily on volunteers in a huge range of areas. There’s something for everyone – Cornish Hill just might be what you’re looking for.

Friends of CH is a small group of people who want this piece of bush habitat to be enjoyed by all. Unfortunately there’s nasty weeds which need our attention along the main track which follows Smiths Creek. Over many years we’ve planted natives which have mostly survived but we need to weed around them and adjust the tree guards.

If you can help, we’re recommencing our Sunday working bees . PLEASE COME ALONG for what ever time you can afford. Don’t leave it to someone else !!!

  • Sunday March 20
  • Sunday May 15
  • Sunday June 19

All working bees are from 10 am until noon.

Park at the end of Stanley St and then stroll down to the picnic table.

BYO gloves, appropriate clothes, water bottle, hat and sun screen.

For inquiries contact Jan – janthommo53@gmail.com.
