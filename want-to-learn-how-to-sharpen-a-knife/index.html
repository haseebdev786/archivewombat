Want to Learn How to Sharpen a Knife?

May 14, 2021 | Living well | 0

During 2021 Daylesford Repair Cafe is running a series of ‘how-to’ workshops so you can learn how to fix things and keep things in good working order.

Have you got knives and tools that need sharpening? Do you want to learn how to sharpen them yourself so they remain in excellent working order? Come along to our introductory workshop and we’ll teach you how.

Your tutor Mark Hallett is a master at sharpening. He is a regular fixer at the Repair Café with additional skills in woodwork, metal work and jewellery.

What: Knife and tool sharpening workshop

When: Sunday May 16th, 1.30-3.30pm

Where: Victoria Park Pavillion, Ballan Road, Daylesford

Organised by: Daylesford Repair Cafe

Cost: Gold coin donation

Join us at the Repair Cafe between 1pm and 4pm this Sunday, May 16th, and see what else our team of fixers are capable of repairing. Enjoy a cuppa, cake and conversation while you’re there. In addition to our knife and tool sharpening workshop, there’ll be fixers able to repair a range of household items and gardening tools including mechanical and electrical problems, as well as mending your favourite items of clothing. There’s also bike repairs plus our newly established ‘mending broken hearts’ table for those in need of some tender loving care.

For further information contact Nikki Marshall (0432 232 073) or Danny Kinnear (0488 604 231) or check out the Daylesford Repair Cafe Facebook page.
