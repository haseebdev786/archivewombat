What’s on at the Neighbourhood Centre

Feb 4, 2022 | News | 0

Term one has kicked off at the Daylesford Neighbourhood Centre. There are a range of health related classes including Yoga, Silken Movements, Stretch Therapy and Tai Chi for Beginners.

For information on health related classes contact see the Brouchure on the website. Classes are pay as you go. Contact the tutor to book your place:

Yoga: Jan Tepper 0419 121 961

8 Silken Movements and Tai Chi: Gudrun 0490 053 521

Stretch Therapy: John 0438 781 137

Other Courses

POETRY OF ISLAM Join local author Alice Melike Ülgezer exploring the poetic and mystical traditions of Islam. The course opens with an exploration of pre-Islamic Bedouin poetry and moves on to the development of Islam and the mystical traditions, poets and saints of Central Asia, Iran and Turkey. It concludes with some modern Middle Eastern poets and a film screening by a director steeped in the Sufi Poetic.
Commences 19th Feb.

To book a place click here

Introduction to Aromatherapy commences at DNC on 28th Feb. This 5 week course will introduce you to the many practical uses of essential oils and how they can influence the mind and body. Each week, participants will be making a variety of aromatherapy products to take home and enjoy.

This course is an ACFE sponsored course and eligiblity criteria applies.

Cost is $180 full price, or $150 concession. Cost includes $35 of material cost per student. Commences 28th Feb

To book a place click here

Responsible serving of alcohol. This is an official Victorian Liquor Licensing Commission program designed to give participants the skills and knowledge necessary to ensure a safe environment in licensed premises. Now is a great time to upskill and get yourself job-ready. Course Date: Feb 25. Cost $110
