Winter Darning Workshop at Daylesford Repair Cafe

Jul 18, 2025 | News | 0

Welcome to Winter and an opportunity to check out what’s around the house, garage, workshop etc in need of repairing, sharpening or mending.

This coming Repair Café will include a workshop on darning an item of clothing that needs repairing. Come along and learn from our expert at Mending, Darning and Sewing, to mention a few of Jackie’s skills and attributes, while enjoying good company, conversation, and a cuppa.

While you are waiting for repairs to be attended to, a cuppa and sweet/savoury delight can be enjoyed at the Cafe.

A donation to cover our expenses is always appreciated for the completed repair or cuppa.

Date: Sunday 20th July from
Time: 1.00pm to 4.00pm
Venue: Victoria Park Pavilion (on the Daylesford-Ballan Rd)

The Daylesford Repair Café flags will be out the front.
