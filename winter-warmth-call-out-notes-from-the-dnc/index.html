Winter Warmth Call Out – Notes from the DNC

May 9, 2025 | News | 0

Help keep less fortunate member of the community warm this winter

As the cold sets in, many in our community are facing winter without the basics to stay warm. We’re collecting and distributing winter essentials to support those feeling the cold.

What we need:

  • Warm blankets
  • Coats & jackets
  • Beanies, scarves & gloves
  • Socks (new only, please!)
  • Sleeping bags
  • Other winter basics to help people stay dry and warm

If you’ve got spares tucked away in a cupboard – or you’re able to donate something new – every item makes a difference.

Drop-off location: Daylesford Neighbourhood Centre
Times: Mon-Thurs 9am-3pm
Enquiries: 5342 3569

Let’s wrap our community in warmth this winter. Thanks for caring.
