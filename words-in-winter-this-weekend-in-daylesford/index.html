Words in Winter – This Weekend in Daylesford!

Aug 25, 2023 | Living well | 0

Dead poets, live poets, poetry readings in cosy, indie bookshops…

Puppetry workshops and birdsongs…

Authors discussing their craft – getting your story out, the pitfalls of working with co-authors, the highs, the lows…

From crime thrillers to horror to grief to writing about social justice…

And an open mike event with stories, songs, poetry and music around the hearth.

It’s all happening at Words in Winter this weekend. There are lots of free events and some very well known authors.

Do yourself a favour, explore the full Words in Winter program, and get to one or more of the events this weekend.
