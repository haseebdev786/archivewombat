This church, nestled in the bush, is filled with history. It was built by locals in 1875 using bricks made from clay dug from the neighbouring dairy farm which were then fired in a hand-made kiln.

The church has excellent acoustics, perfect for musical concerts and spoken word. The raked floor makes for easy viewing and listening and the stained-glass windows frame the surrounding eucalypts outside.

The Yandoit, Clydesdale and Franklinford Community Planning Group have negotiated an agreement with the Uniting Church to keep this beautiful and historical building in community hands, at least until the end of 2023. This is an alternative to private sale, which has been the fate of so many historical churches in our local area.
