Youth Mental Health First Aid

Mar 5, 2021 | News, Living well | 0

A free Youth Mental Health First Aid workshop will be offered for adults working with young people in the Shire. Similar to regular First Aid, Youth Mental Health First Aid is an accredited course to train people to assist young people experiencing mental health conditions.

The 14-hour Youth Mental Health First Aid Course is for adults such as school staff, parents, sports coaches, community group leaders and youth workers who work with, live with or care for adolescents.

The workshop is being sponsored by Hepburn Shire Council and Youth Gurus.
